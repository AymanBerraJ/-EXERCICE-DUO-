Il était une fois dans un monde fantastique, an old wise man lived in a far land behind the mountains where he and his family had a simple yet happy life.
Un beau matin, ce vieil homme que l'on va appeler Billy décida d'aller en pleine forêt pour y chasser des lapins carnivore. va-t-il chasser ou se faire chasser?
Malheureusement il va se faire chasser. Il décide de s'abriter dans une grotte et d'attendre que le soleil se couche pour pouvoir prendre la route du retour afin d'éviter
ces lapins.
He went into the dark cold forest with only a lamp and a knife all alone.
Il est revenu avec un bras en moins. On his way home he met the king who was going hunting with his son, both of them were shocked at the fact that this old man went alone into the forest to bring some meat to his family, they gave him two hundred kilos of gold because of his courage and he lived happily ever after.
